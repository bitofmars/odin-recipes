Greek Omelette

Description

This delicious Greek omelette filled with chunks of feta cheese and aromatic mint is perfect for any meal.

Ingredients:

  • 3 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • A pinch of salt
  • A handful of fresh chopped mint
  • 2 tablespoons crumbled feta
  • 1/2 teaspoon olive oil
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. In a bowl beat the eggs with all the ingredients except the olive oil.
  2. Heat (low heat) the olive oil in a heavy medium sized pan.
  3. Add the egg mixture making sure it spreads evenly on the whole pan.
  4. Let it cook until it starts to get solid but still liquid on top.
  5. Carefully fold in half with a rubber spatula.
